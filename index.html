Bristol’s meet-up group for everyone who loves fonts & lettering

We are lettering artists, signpainters, typeface designers, letterpress printers, calligraphers, typographers, tattooists, font technologists, font fans, and more. We get together for drinks and networking every two months.

Letter Luvvers events start with short talks, then people stay as long as they like. They’re on weekday evenings, they’re free, and they’re usually in a pub. If you’d like to come, please sign up on EventBrite so we can judge numbers and contact you in case of important updates. Follow us on Eventbrite, Instagram or Discord to hear about future events. There’s no profit motive, just bringing people of related passions together.
